Diplomat Business Club in association with LWK Group
The Diplomat Business Club is a UK based global network connecting distinguished Business Leaders, Diplomats, Government officials, CEOs & Investors and other eminent members of society. Their high-profile events are attended by members of Royal family, Presidents, Ministers, Business & Corporate Leaders and Family Offices representing various countries and creating unique networking opportunities.
